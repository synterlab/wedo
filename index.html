🕹 How to Play

1
Press START DROP

An energy sphere appears at the top of the board and falls through the crystal peg network.

2
Watch the landing zone

As the sphere falls, the zone bar below lights up — showing which zone the ball is heading toward.

3
Ball lands → asset revealed

A scanning animation plays, then an asset with a specific rarity tier is revealed.

4
Claim your asset

Press ⚡ CLAIM to save the asset to your collection. More drops = more discoveries!

🔥 Streak System

  • Drop 3× in a row with Rare/Hidden/Legendary results → STREAK activated!
  • Each streak drop adds a score multiplier (×3, ×4, …)
  • Streak breaks if you land TRENDING or HOT
  • Longer streak = bigger score bonus

🌌 What is WEDO?

WEDO is an onchain discovery arcade game built on the Orynth ecosystem. Every drop simulates the discovery of an asset in the crypto market — full of surprises, risk, and rewards.

This digital Plinko mechanic mirrors the unpredictability of asset movements in the market. You can't control the outcome, but you can build your collection strategy.

❓ FAQ

Is this a real blockchain game?

WEDO is currently an arcade demo — assets and scores are simulated. The onchain version is in development within the Orynth ecosystem.

What assets can be discovered?

There are 10 assets in the Orynth ecosystem: VOID, WAVE, NOVA, ECHO, FLUX, ZERO, ATOM, GHOST, ORBIT, and CORE.

How do I get on the leaderboard?

Accumulate score from each drop. Higher rarity + longer streak = higher score.

Can I mute the sound?

Yes! Tap the speaker icon in the top right corner to mute/unmute game sound effects.
